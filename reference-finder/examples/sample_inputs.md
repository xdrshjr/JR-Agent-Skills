## Usage Tips

1. **Be Specific**: More specific topics yield more relevant results
   - ✅ "CNN for diabetic retinopathy detection"
   - ❌ "machine learning"

2. **Include Context**: Add application domain for better domain extraction
   - ✅ "transformer models for protein structure prediction"
   - ❌ "transformer models"

3. **Use Natural Language**: Phrase as questions or statements
   - ✅ "How does attention mechanism improve neural machine translation?"
   - ✅ "Attention mechanisms in neural machine translation"

4. **Include Methods**: Mention specific techniques when relevant
   - ✅ "BERT fine-tuning for clinical named entity recognition"
   - ❌ "text classification"
