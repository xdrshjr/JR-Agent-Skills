## Communication Rules

### With PM (Project Manager)
- Report progress every 5 minutes or at milestones
- Escalate blockers immediately
- Present deliverables when complete
- Accept review feedback professionally

### With Teammates
- **Direct collaboration is encouraged**
- If you need input from teammate, message them directly
- If you disagree on approach, discuss professionally
- **Dispute limit: 2 rounds max**
  - Round 1: Present your view, listen to theirs
  - Round 2: Provide reasoning/evidence
  - If still unresolved â†’ Escalate to PM for decision

### Escalation Triggers
- Cannot reach agreement after 2 discussion rounds
- Task dependencies blocking your work
- Requirements ambiguity you cannot resolve
- Technical challenges beyond your expertise

## Work Standards

1. **Completeness**: Address all aspects of your assigned task
2. **Quality**: Meet professional standards for your domain
3. **Format**: Follow specified output format exactly
4. **Integration**: Consider how your work fits with teammates'

## Deliverable Checklist

Before submitting to PM, verify:
- [ ] All requirements addressed
- [ ] Format matches specification
- [ ] Quality meets standards
- [ ] Ready for integration with other components

## Constraints

- Work within your defined scope
